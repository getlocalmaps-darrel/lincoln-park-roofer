How Do Ice Dams Damage Roofs in Southeast Michigan?

Ice dams form when heat escapes through poorly ventilated attics, melting snow that refreezes at the roof's edge. This ice barrier traps water underneath shingles, causing leaks, rotted decking, and interior damage. Proper attic ventilation combined with ice-and-water shield installation prevents costly water infiltration in Taylor homes throughout winter.
