If damage is isolated (missing shingles, flashing issues, a small leak), roof repair may be enough. If shingles are brittle, curling, failing in multiple areas, or the roof is near end-of-life, roof replacement is often the better long-term value. We’ll inspect and show you what we see.
Cost depends on roof size, pitch, tear-off needs, decking condition, ventilation, and materials. We keep pricing competitive and provide free estimates with a clear scope of work.
