ABout The City of Manvel Texas

Texas's Manvel, a thriving and expanding municipality, is found just south of Houston. When compared to comparable cities with a population of 10,000 or more, Pearland had the 15th quickest growth from 2000 to 2010 with a population increase of 142%. Since 2000, Manvel has been the third-largest and fastest-growing city in the Houston MSA. In 2017, the City of Pearland's population was estimated to be approximately 120,000, and when the ETJ is taken into account, that number rises to around 144,000.
