## Common Roof Repair Issues in Grosse Ile Homes
- Active roof leaks: visible water stains on ceilings or attic decking
- Missing or curling shingles: often caused by wind gusts and aging 3-tab asphalt
- Damaged flashing: around chimneys, skylights, and roof-wall transitions where most leaks start
- Ice dam damage: shingle lifting and fascia rot from winter freeze-thaw cycles
- Storm-damaged valleys: torn underlayment and displaced ridge caps after high-wind events
- Failed pipe boots & vents: cracked rubber collars that drip directly into the attic

## High-Wind & Storm Damage Repair on Grosse Ile
Grosse Ile is the only true island community in Downriver — surrounded by open Detroit River water on all sides with no surrounding tree line, no neighboring buildings, and no terrain to break incoming wind. Sustained 30–45 mph gusts during normal frontal passages are routine; 60+ mph gusts during summer derechos and winter Alberta clippers are common. Grosse Ile shingles fail at the rate of inland roofs that are 5–7 years older.

What sustained high-wind exposure does to Grosse Ile roofs:
- **Lifted and creased shingles** at the upwind eave — usually the south or west exposure facing the river
- **Torn underlayment and displaced ridge caps** after a single storm event
- **Shingle blow-off** in 30+ mph sustained gusts when the original adhesive strip has aged past 8–10 years
- **Pulled flashing and step counter-flashing** at chimney, sidewall, and skylight penetrations
- **Gutter and downspout damage** from windborne debris off open river fetch

What we install on Grosse Ile to actually hold up:
- **Owens Corning Duration Storm shingles** with the SureNail strip — rated to 130 mph wind warranty
- **6-nail high-wind installation pattern** instead of the standard 4-nail (industry minimum)
- **Full ice & water shield** at all eaves, valleys, rakes, and penetrations — not just code-minimum 3 ft
- **Stainless or copper flashing** where standard galvanized would corrode in the river-borne salt air
- **Written workmanship warranty + manufacturer wind warranty** on every Grosse Ile repair
