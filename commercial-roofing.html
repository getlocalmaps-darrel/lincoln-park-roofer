Professional Commercial Roofing Services in Lincoln Park, Michigan

Lincoln Park has a lot of small businesses, strip malls, and light industrial buildings along Fort Street and Dix Avenue — and most of them have flat or low-slope roofs. These commercial roof systems are completely different from residential shingles. They require different materials, different installation techniques, and different maintenance schedules. Lincoln Park Roofing handles both sides, and we bring the same attention to a 3,000 sq ft retail flat roof as we do to a 20,000 sq ft warehouse. If you already have a leak, see our commercial roof repair page.

One thing commercial building owners in Downriver often overlook: ponding water. Michigan gets 34 inches of rain per year plus snow and ice. Flat roofs that don't drain properly develop standing water that degrades the membrane, adds structural load, and eventually leaks through. Every commercial roof we install includes proper drainage design — tapered insulation, internal drains or scuppers, and enough slope to keep water moving. For buildings that need a dedicated flat roof system, we also offer flat roof installation with multiple membrane options.

Commercial Roofing Services We Provide in Lincoln Park

  • TPO Roof Installation – Thermoplastic polyolefin membranes are energy-efficient, UV-resistant, and handle Michigan's temperature swings well. We heat-weld every seam for a watertight bond.
  • EPDM Rubber Roofing – EPDM is a proven commercial roofing membrane that handles extreme cold without cracking. Great for Lincoln Park warehouses and retail buildings.
  • Modified Bitumen Systems – For low-slope commercial roofs that need heavy foot traffic resistance, modified bitumen provides excellent waterproofing and durability.
  • Standing Seam Metal – Long-lasting, low-maintenance metal roofing for commercial buildings that want 40+ year performance and a clean appearance.
  • Preventative Maintenance Programs – Twice-yearly inspections, drain clearing, seam checks, and minor repairs before they become major problems. Saves thousands over the roof's life.
  • Commercial Roof Coatings – Silicone and acrylic coatings extend the life of existing flat roofs by 10-15 years at a fraction of replacement cost.

What Is the Best Commercial Roofing System for Michigan?

TPO and EPDM are the two most common commercial roofing membranes in Southeast Michigan. TPO is energy-efficient with a white reflective surface and handles UV well. EPDM is a black rubber membrane that excels in extreme cold and costs less upfront. For Lincoln Park businesses, either system performs well when installed correctly with proper insulation and drainage.

TPO vs. EPDM — Commercial Roofing Membranes

Choosing between TPO and EPDM for your Lincoln Park commercial building depends on your budget, energy goals, and building use.

Pro Tip: Schedule Commercial Roof Inspections Twice a Year

Commercial flat roofs need inspection in spring (after winter ice damage) and fall (before winter sets in). Catching a small membrane tear or clogged drain now prevents a $15,000 emergency repair in February. We offer maintenance programs that include twice-yearly inspections and priority scheduling for any issues we find.

Commercial Roofing FAQs — Lincoln Park, MI

Quick answers to the most common commercial roofing questions from Lincoln Park business owners.

What types of commercial roofing do you install in Lincoln Park?

We install TPO, EPDM rubber, modified bitumen, and standing seam metal systems. Each has different strengths — we'll recommend the best fit based on your building type, budget, and performance needs.

How much does commercial roofing cost in Lincoln Park, MI?

Commercial roofing costs in Lincoln Park typically range from $5 to $12 per square foot installed, depending on the membrane type, insulation requirements, and building complexity. A 5,000 sq ft flat roof might run $25,000 to $60,000. We provide free estimates.

How long does a commercial roof last in Michigan?

TPO and EPDM systems last 20-30 years with proper maintenance. Standing seam metal can last 40+ years. Michigan's freeze-thaw cycles are hard on all roofing, which is why maintenance programs matter for commercial buildings.

Do you offer commercial roof maintenance programs in Lincoln Park?

Yes. Our maintenance program includes twice-yearly inspections, drain clearing, seam checks, and minor repairs. This catches problems early and extends your roof's lifespan significantly. Most commercial building owners recoup the cost many times over.

Can you work on my Lincoln Park business without shutting down operations?

Absolutely. We plan commercial jobs to minimize disruption. For retail and office buildings, we can stage materials early and work during off-hours. For warehouse and industrial buildings, we coordinate with your team to avoid conflicts with loading and operations.

Do you handle commercial roof insurance claims in Lincoln Park?

Yes. Storm damage to commercial roofs follows the same claim process as residential. We document damage, provide scopes, meet adjusters, and file supplements when needed. We've handled commercial claims on buildings throughout Wayne County.
