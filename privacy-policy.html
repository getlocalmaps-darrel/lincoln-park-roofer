Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Lincoln Park Roofing ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through our website at lincolnparkroofing.com, how we use it, and how we protect it.

Information We Collect

When you fill out a contact form, request an estimate, or call us through the website, we may collect:

  • Your name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Home address or general location (for scheduling estimates)
  • Details about your roofing project (roof type, issue description, etc.)

We only collect information that you voluntarily provide to us. We do not collect sensitive personal information like Social Security numbers, financial account details, or health information.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and contact requests
  • Schedule on-site inspections and estimates
  • Follow up on roofing projects and service requests
  • Send project updates if you've requested work from us

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Period.

Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies for basic site functionality and analytics:

  • Google Analytics -- We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website (which pages are popular, how long people stay, what devices they use). Google Analytics collects anonymous, aggregated data. It does not identify you personally.
  • Essential cookies -- Basic cookies that help the website function properly (session management, remembering your preferences).

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. The website will still work, but some features may be limited.

Third-Party Services

Our website uses a few third-party services:

  • Google Maps -- We embed a Google Maps iframe on some pages to show our location and service area. Google's own privacy policy applies to data collected through their maps widget.
  • Google Analytics -- For anonymous website usage statistics. See Google's privacy policy.
  • Google Fonts -- We load fonts (Oswald and Open Sans) from Google's servers. Google may log basic connection data when fonts are loaded.
  • Font Awesome (Cloudflare CDN) -- We load icons from Cloudflare's content delivery network. Cloudflare's privacy policy governs their data handling.

Data Retention

We keep your contact information for as long as it's needed to complete your roofing project and handle any warranty or follow-up questions. For general inquiries that don't lead to a project, we typically retain your information for up to 24 months, then delete it.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the personal information you share with us. Our website uses HTTPS encryption for all connections. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Ask what personal information we have about you
  • Request that we correct inaccurate information
  • Request that we delete your personal information
  • Opt out of any future communications from us

To exercise any of these rights, just give us a call or send us an email (contact details below). We'll take care of it promptly.

Michigan Consumer Protection

We operate in compliance with applicable Michigan consumer protection laws, including the Michigan Consumer Protection Act (MCPA). We are committed to fair and transparent business practices in all of our interactions with Michigan homeowners.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
