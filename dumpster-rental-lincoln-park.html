Common Dumpster Questions

Answers specific to Lincoln Park (48146)

What is the best residential dumpster for Lincoln Park?

We strongly recommend a Rubber Wheeled Trailer. Unlike heavy commercial roll-off dumpsters with steel wheels, rubber tires will not crack your concrete or damage your pavers.

Do I need a permit for a dumpster?

If you place the dumpster on the street, you need a permit from the City. However, Kincaide's trailers fit in most driveways, avoiding the permit requirement entirely.

What items are not allowed?

You cannot dispose of hazardous materials like paint, tires, batteries, or asbestos. General construction debris, roofing shingles, and furniture are fully accepted.

How much does a 20-yard dumpster cost?

Prices fluctuate, but expect to pay between $400 and $500 for a 20-yard rental. Kincaide offers flat-rate pricing with no surprise fees.
