Cookie Preferences

Last updated: March 2026

This page explains what cookies and similar technologies our website uses, why we use them, and how you can manage your preferences.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites store on your device when you visit. They help websites remember your preferences, understand how you use the site, and improve your experience. Some cookies are set by us, and others are set by third-party services we use.

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to work properly. They handle things like:

  • Keeping the site functioning correctly during your visit
  • Remembering your session as you navigate between pages
  • Basic security features

Essential cookies cannot be disabled without breaking core site functionality.

Analytics Cookies (Google Analytics)

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. This helps us know which pages are popular, how long people spend on the site, and what devices and browsers our visitors use. Google Analytics collects this data anonymously -- it does not identify you personally.

Information collected by Google Analytics includes:

  • Pages you visit and how long you spend on each page
  • How you arrived at our site (search engine, direct visit, referral)
  • Your general geographic area (city-level, not your exact address)
  • Your browser type and device category (desktop, mobile, tablet)

To opt out of Google Analytics tracking, you can install the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on. This works across all websites, not just ours.

Third-Party Cookies and Services

Our website loads resources from a few third-party services. Each of these services may set their own cookies according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google Maps -- We embed Google Maps on some pages to show our location and service area. Google may set cookies when the map loads. See Google's privacy policy.
  • Google Fonts -- We load the Oswald and Open Sans fonts from Google's servers. Google may log basic connection data (IP address, browser type) when fonts load. No tracking cookies are set by Google Fonts.
  • Font Awesome (Cloudflare CDN) -- We load icons from Cloudflare's content delivery network. Cloudflare may set performance-related cookies. See Cloudflare's privacy policy.
  • Tailwind CSS (CDN) -- We load a CSS framework from a CDN for styling. This is a static resource load and does not set tracking cookies.

How to Manage Cookies in Your Browser

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Here's how for the most common browsers:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions > Manage and delete cookies

Keep in mind that disabling all cookies may affect how the website works. Essential features like form submissions and page navigation should still function, but some elements may not display correctly.

Changes to This Page

If we add new cookies or change how we use existing ones, we'll update this page and the "Last updated" date at the top.
