Accessibility Statement

Last reviewed: March 2026

Lincoln Park Roofing is committed to making our website accessible to everyone, including people with disabilities. We believe every homeowner in Downriver Michigan should be able to learn about our roofing services, read our reviews, and contact us without barriers.

Our Accessibility Goal

We aim to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at Level AA. These are the widely accepted standards for web accessibility, and they guide how we design and maintain our site.

What We've Done

Here are the accessibility features built into our website:

  • Semantic HTML -- We use proper heading hierarchy (H1, H2, H3), landmark elements, and structured content so screen readers can navigate the site effectively.
  • Alt text on images -- All meaningful images include descriptive alt text. Decorative images are marked as such so screen readers skip them.
  • Keyboard navigation -- You can navigate through the entire site using only a keyboard. All interactive elements (links, buttons, menus) are accessible via Tab, Enter, and arrow keys.
  • Color contrast -- Text and background color combinations meet WCAG AA contrast ratios for readability. Our brand blue (#0056b3) on white backgrounds exceeds the 4.5:1 ratio required for normal text.
  • Mobile-responsive design -- The site adapts to all screen sizes, from phones to desktops. Content reflows without horizontal scrolling at up to 400% zoom.
  • Focus indicators -- Interactive elements show visible focus outlines when navigated by keyboard.
  • ARIA labels -- Buttons and links that use icons instead of text include ARIA labels describing their purpose.
  • Readable fonts -- We use Open Sans for body text and Oswald for headings -- both are clean, legible typefaces at comfortable sizes.

Known Limitations

We want to be upfront about a few things that may affect accessibility:

  • Tailwind CSS loading -- Our site loads the Tailwind CSS framework via JavaScript. During the brief moment before it loads (usually under 1 second), screen readers may encounter unstyled content. The content itself remains fully accessible and readable.
  • Google Maps embed -- We include a Google Maps iframe to show our location and service area. This embedded content is controlled by Google, and its accessibility features are limited to what Google provides. The same location information (address, phone number) is available in text form on every page.
  • PDF documents -- If we link to any PDF documents (proposals, warranties), they may not be fully accessible. Contact us and we'll provide the information in an alternative format.

Third-Party Content

Our website includes content from third-party services that we don't fully control:

  • Google Maps (location embed)
  • Google Fonts (Oswald and Open Sans typefaces)
  • Font Awesome via Cloudflare CDN (icons)

While we choose third-party services that take accessibility seriously, we can't guarantee their compliance. If you have trouble accessing any part of our site because of third-party content, let us know and we'll help you get the information you need.

Reporting Accessibility Issues

If you run into any accessibility barriers on our website, we want to hear about it. Tell us what page you were on, what you were trying to do, and what happened. We'll work to fix the issue as quickly as we can.

Ongoing Efforts

Accessibility isn't a one-time project -- it's something we work on continuously. We regularly review our website for accessibility issues, test with keyboard-only navigation, and update our code to follow current best practices. When we add new pages or features, accessibility is part of the process from the start.
