Roof Replacement in Lincoln Park

March 03, 2026 · · By Scott
A complete roof replacement in Lincoln Park, MI costs between $8,000 and $15,000 for most residential homes. At Lincoln Park Roofing, we replaced a storm-damaged roof using GAF Timberline HDZ shingles in just 3 days, installing 30 squares with upgraded underlayment to withstand Michigan's freeze-thaw cycles and summer storms.

Project Overview

The homeowner contacted us after a severe windstorm ripped off 14 shingles and exposed the decking near their chimney. We inspected the entire roof and found compromised OSB decking in two sections totaling 120 square feet. At Lincoln Park Roofing, we replaced 30 squares of roofing with GAF Timberline HDZ shingles in Charcoal, installed new Ice & Water Shield along all eaves and valleys, and replaced damaged decking with 7/16-inch OSB. The project took 3 days from tearoff to final cleanup, and the homeowner was relieved to have a watertight roof before the next forecast. We serve homeowners throughout Lincoln Park and provide the same thorough inspection process for every roofer in Lincoln Park project we complete.

Challenges & Solutions

The biggest challenge was discovering that 8 rafters had moisture damage from years of ice dam backup along the north-facing eave. Most homeowners don't realize that Lincoln Park's position along the Ecorse Creek floodplain creates higher humidity levels that accelerate wood rot. We sistered 2x6 boards alongside each damaged rafter before installing new decking, adding 1 day to the timeline but preventing future structural issues. We also upgraded the homeowner to GAF's FeltBuster synthetic underlayment instead of standard felt because it handles Michigan's temperature swings better — it won't crack at -10°F like traditional felt paper.

Pro Tip from Lincoln Park Roofing: Check your attic after every heavy snow. We find that 60% of Lincoln Park homes lack adequate soffit and ridge venting, which causes ice dams when attic temps hit 40°F while it's 20°F outside. If you see frost on the underside of your roof decking in January, you need ventilation upgrades before your next roof replacement — we install 1 square foot of net free ventilation area per 150 square feet of attic space to match Michigan code.
