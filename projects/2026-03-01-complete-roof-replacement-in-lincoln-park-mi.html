Complete Roof Replacement in Lincoln Park, MI

March 01, 2026 · Fort Street area, Lincoln Park, MI · By Scott
We completed a full roof replacement in the Fort Street area of Lincoln Park, MI, using high-quality architectural shingles. The result is a beautiful, durable roof that enhances the home’s curb appeal and protects it from Michigan's unpredictable weather.

Project Overview

The homeowners in the Fort Street area needed a complete roof replacement due to age and wear from Michigan's harsh winters and storms. We assessed the roof's condition and recommended high-quality architectural shingles for their durability and aesthetic appeal. The project involved stripping the old roof down to the decking, which measured approximately 1,800 square feet. We installed Owens Corning Duration shingles, known for their longevity and weather resistance. The installation took just three days, with our in-house crew ensuring meticulous attention to detail. At Lincoln Park Roofing, we pride ourselves on delivering top-notch roof replacement services in Lincoln Park and the surrounding areas.

Challenges & Solutions

One unique challenge we faced during this project was the underlying decking's condition, which had suffered minor water damage over the years. Before proceeding with the new shingles, we carefully replaced the damaged sections to ensure a solid foundation. This step is crucial as many homeowners underestimate the importance of a sturdy decking beneath their roofs. By addressing these issues upfront, we ensured the longevity and effectiveness of the new roof system.

Pro Tip from Lincoln Park Roofing: When selecting shingles, look for options with a high algae resistance rating to prevent unsightly streaks over time. Owens Corning Duration provides this feature, ensuring your roof stays looking fresh long after installation.

About Fort Street area

The Fort Street area of Lincoln Park, MI, is known for its charming residential homes that often feature classic architectural styles. With a mix of older and newer properties, homeowners here face unique roofing challenges, particularly due to the Michigan climate. High winds and heavy snow can take a toll on roofs, making regular maintenance and timely replacements essential. The community is also subject to HOA guidelines that may dictate roofing materials, so it’s vital for homeowners to choose options that not only meet these requirements but also enhance their property’s curb appeal.

Residential Roofing Details

A complete roof replacement in Lincoln Park typically involves removing the existing roofing materials down to the decking, which is crucial for assessing any underlying damage.
