Roof Replacement in Lincoln Park, MI

March 03, 2026 · · By Scott
Lincoln Park Roofing installed GAF Timberline HDZ shingles on a 1,800 square foot home suffering from 15-year-old wind damage and underlayment deterioration. Project Overview

The homeowner contacted us after noticing granule loss and curling shingles across their south-facing roof sections—common damage from Lincoln Park's freeze-thaw cycles and summer heat. We discovered the original felt underlayment had failed in multiple spots, violating current Wayne County building codes. At Lincoln Park Roofing, we stripped the roof down to the decking, replaced 14 sheets of compromised OSB sheathing, and installed GAF FeltBuster synthetic underlayment rated for 30-year protection. We chose GAF Timberline HDZ shingles in Weathered Wood for their StrikeZone nailing area and LayerLock technology, which resists Michigan's 60+ mph wind gusts. Our crew finished the installation in 72 hours, hauled away 42 bundles of old material, and left the property cleaner than we found it. According to Lincoln Park Roofing field reports, homes within 2 miles of Fort Park see 23% more shingle deterioration due to industrial air particulates—this replacement will protect the structure for the next 25-30 years.

Challenges & Solutions

We found the original underlayment had completely deteriorated around the chimney flashing and along the northern valleys—areas where ice dams form during Lincoln Park winters. Most homeowners don't realize that shingles can look acceptable while the underlayment underneath is failing. We tore off the compromised layers and installed GAF FeltBuster synthetic underlayment, which resists moisture 5 times better than traditional felt paper. We also replaced corroded step flashing around the brick chimney using copper-coated galvanized metal rated for 50-year durability. This prevents the slow leaks that rot roof decking and require $3,000-$5,000 structural repairs down the line.

Pro Tip from Lincoln Park Roofing: Check your attic after heavy snow for frost accumulation on the underside of roof decking. If you see it, your ventilation system is failing—warm air is escaping and condensing, which rots sheathing and reduces GAF Timberline HDZ lifespan by 8-12 years. We install continuous ridge vents paired with soffit intake vents at a 1:150 ratio to maintain proper airflow and keep your warranty valid.
