Project Overview

The homeowner in New Boston needed to extend the life of their approximately 20-year-old three-tab roof, which was still holding up but showing signs of age. They approached us at Lincoln Park Roofing for a roof rejuvenation, aiming for an additional three to five years of protection. According to my assessment, the roof was a good candidate for rejuvenation. We utilized Owens Corning's specialized rejuvenation products, which not only restored the shingle flexibility but also helped protect against future weather damage. The entire process took less than a day, and the homeowner was thrilled with the results. At Lincoln Park Roofing, we focus on delivering quality roof rejuvenation in New Boston and surrounding areas.

Challenges & Solutions

The main challenge was ensuring that the existing shingles had enough integrity to be rejuvenated. Many homeowners believe that once a roof reaches a certain age, it must be replaced, but that isn't always true. I found that the shingles still had significant life left and provided the homeowner with a cost-effective solution to extend their roof's lifespan. Utilizing Owens Corning's rejuvenation products allowed us to restore flexibility and waterproofing, enhancing the roof's resistance to Michigan's unpredictable weather.

Pro Tip from Lincoln Park Roofing: If your roof is showing signs of aging, don’t wait for leaks to appear. A roof rejuvenation can be a cost-effective solution to prolong the life of your shingles. We’ve seen roofs last well beyond their expected lifespan with this treatment, especially when using Owens Corning products.
