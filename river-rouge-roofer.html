When Should You Replace Your Roof Instead of Repairing It?

Replace your roof when damage is widespread, shingles are curling or cracking across large sections, the decking shows signs of rot, or the roof has gone through multiple repair cycles. If damage is isolated to a small area — a few missing shingles or a single flashing leak — a targeted repair is usually more cost-effective. A free inspection from our crew will clarify which option makes the most sense for your River Rouge home.
