Roof Replacement & New Roof Construction FAQ

Answers to common questions homeowners ask about roof replacement in Lincoln Park, Michigan.

Most homeowners use the terms interchangeably. Roof replacement usually means tearing off old shingles and installing a new roofing system. New roof construction can also apply to new builds or major rebuilds. We’ll confirm the right scope after an inspection.
Roof replacement pricing depends on square footage, pitch, tear-off, decking condition, ventilation, and the shingles you choose. Because we’re local and run an efficient crew, many customers find our pricing very competitive compared to larger contractors. Call for a free estimate for an exact quote.
Most roof replacements can be completed in 1–2 days. Complex roof lines, steep pitches, chimney/valley details, or decking repairs can add time. We give you a realistic timeline before work starts.
In most cases, a full tear-off is the best long-term option because it lets us inspect decking and install the full roofing system properly. We’ll tell you what’s best for your roof and budget.
Yes. During tear-off we inspect the decking and replace any soft, rotted, or damaged sections so your new roof has a solid base.
We install quality asphalt shingle systems with the right underlayment, ice & water shield, flashing, and ventilation. We’ll recommend options based on your roof shape, ventilation needs, and budget.
Ventilation is one of the most important parts of a durable roofing system. We evaluate intake/exhaust ventilation and recommend improvements to reduce heat and moisture buildup through Michigan’s seasons.
Yes. Warranty coverage depends on the materials and system selected. We explain warranty options during your estimate so you know exactly what’s covered.
