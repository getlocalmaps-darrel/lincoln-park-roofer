Roof Replacement & New Roof Construction FAQ

Answers to common questions homeowners ask about roof replacement in Lincoln Park, Michigan.

Most homeowners use the terms interchangeably. Roof replacement usually means tearing off old shingles and installing a new roofing system. New roof construction can also apply to new builds or major rebuilds. We’ll confirm the right scope after an inspection.
Roof replacement pricing depends on square footage, pitch, tear-off, decking condition, ventilation, and the shingles you choose. Because we’re local and run an efficient crew, many customers find our pricing very competitive compared to larger contractors. Call for a free estimate for an exact quote.
Most residential roof replacements in Lincoln Park can be completed in one to two days, weather permitting. Complex roof lines, steep pitches, chimney or valley details, and decking repairs can add time. We always give you a realistic timeline before work starts so there are no surprises.
In most cases, a full tear-off is the best long-term option because it lets us inspect the wood decking underneath and install the complete roofing system properly — underlayment, ice and water shield, flashing, and ventilation. We'll evaluate your roof and recommend whichever approach makes the most sense for your home and budget.
Yes. During tear-off we inspect every section of wood decking and replace any soft, rotted, or water-damaged boards so your new roofing system has a solid, structurally sound base. This is one of the most important steps in a roof replacement and something we never skip.
We install quality asphalt shingle roofing systems with proper underlayment, ice and water shield along eaves and valleys, step and counter flashing, and balanced ridge ventilation. For Lincoln Park homes dealing with Michigan's freeze-thaw cycles, we recommend materials built to handle heavy wind, ice, and temperature swings. We'll walk you through options that fit your roof shape, ventilation needs, and budget.
Ventilation is one of the most important parts of a durable roofing system and is always included in our installs. We evaluate your attic's intake and exhaust airflow and recommend improvements to reduce heat buildup in summer and moisture accumulation in winter. Proper ventilation extends shingle life and helps prevent ice dams — a common problem for Lincoln Park homes.
Yes. Warranty coverage depends on the materials and roofing system you select. We offer manufacturer warranties on shingles and components, plus a workmanship warranty on our installation. During your estimate, we explain all warranty options so you know exactly what's covered and for how long.
