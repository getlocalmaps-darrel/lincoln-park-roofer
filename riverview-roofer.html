How Often Should You Inspect Your Roof in Michigan?

Michigan homeowners should inspect their roof at least twice per year—once in spring after ice dam season and again in late fall before winter arrives. You should also schedule an inspection after any major storm with high winds, hail, or heavy snowfall. Regular inspections catch small problems before they become expensive repairs, especially in Riverview where freeze-thaw cycles and river-proximity moisture accelerate shingle wear.
